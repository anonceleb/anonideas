Privacy Policy

Wordle Optimal Solver (the "Extension") does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) about users.

What the extension does
- Runs locally in your browser to help generate Wordle suggestions.
- When fetching bundled resources (wordlists) the extension may request data from the extension package; these operations happen locally or via the user's browser.

No remote telemetry
- The Extension does not send usage telemetry, analytics, or logs to any external servers.

Third-party services
- If you use any optional integrations, third-party services may have their own privacy policies; read them before enabling.

Contact
- For questions about privacy, open an issue on the project: https://github.com/anonceleb/anonideas
